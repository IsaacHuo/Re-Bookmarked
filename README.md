# Re-Bookmarked

**Re-Bookmarked** is a smart, privacy-friendly Chrome extension that helps you automatically clean, classify, and restructure your browser bookmarks.

Whether your bookmarks are nested too deep, full of duplicates, or just forgotten — Re-Bookmark gives them a second life.

Features:
- Auto-categorize by domain or content
- Detect dead or duplicated links
- Flatten over-nested folders
- Preview before applying changes
- Fully local – no data leaves your browser
